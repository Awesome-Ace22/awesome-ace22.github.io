Project Description

This project aimed to design a cost-effective and user-friendly humidification system to maintain the relative humidity of a music room at optimal levels (40–50% RH) to preserve solid wood guitars. Collaborating with my team, we proposed and evaluated three design concepts, ultimately selecting the Autonomous Mist Diffuser as the final solution. This design uses ultrasonic technology to distribute moisture evenly, ensuring effective humidification while minimizing energy consumption.

Testing Outcomes and Recommendations

Testing verified that the humidifier could achieve 40% RH in just 18 minutes and maintain it for over 14 days. Data trends modeled the efficiency of the ultrasonic mist generation and demonstrated cost savings of 55% compared to the client’s current solution.

This project was a practical exercise in translating client needs into an engineered product, laying a foundation for future work in innovative, sustainable design within mechanical and systems engineering.
